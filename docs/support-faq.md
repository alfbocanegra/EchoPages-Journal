# EchoPages Journal Support & Troubleshooting FAQ

## General
**Q: What platforms does EchoPages Journal support?**
A: EchoPages Journal is available on iOS, Android, web, and desktop (Electron).

**Q: What authentication providers can I use?**
A: You can sign in with Google, Apple, or Dropbox.

## Login & Account
**Q: I can't log in or create an account. What should I do?**
A: Ensure you have a stable internet connection and are using a supported provider. If issues persist, try restarting the app or device.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Password resets are managed by your authentication provider (Google, Apple, Dropbox). Use their recovery tools.

## Sync & Data
**Q: My entries aren't syncing across devices.**
A: Tap "Sync Now" in the app settings. Ensure you are logged in with the same provider on all devices and have internet access.

**Q: How do I export or delete my data?**
A: Use the export/delete options in the app settings. For account deletion, contact support at [support@echopages.app].

## Handwriting Input
**Q: Handwriting input isn't working or is laggy.**
A: Try restarting the app. On mobile, ensure you have the latest version. If using a stylus, check device compatibility.

**Q: Can I export my handwriting as an image?**
A: Yes, use the "Export as PNG" button in the handwriting input screen.

## Media Attachments
**Q: I can't add images, audio, or video.**
A: Ensure the app has permission to access your device's media and storage. Check your device settings.

## Notifications
**Q: I'm not receiving notifications.**
A: Check your device notification settings and ensure notifications are enabled for EchoPages Journal.

## Privacy & Security
**Q: How is my data protected?**
A: All sensitive data is encrypted at rest and in transit. See our [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.md) for details.

**Q: Who can access my data?**
A: Only you can access your journal data. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

## Support
For further help, contact us at [support@echopages.app].
