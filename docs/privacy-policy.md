# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024-06-30

EchoPages Journal ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App across all platforms (web, desktop, and mobile).

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Information:** When you sign up or log in, we collect your email address and authentication tokens from supported providers (Google, Apple, Dropbox).
- **Journal Data:** Your journal entries, media, and metadata are stored securely and encrypted.
- **Usage Data:** We may collect anonymized analytics data to improve the app (e.g., crash reports, feature usage).

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and sync your journal data across devices
- To authenticate you with your chosen provider
- To improve app performance, security, and user experience
- To respond to support requests

## 3. Data Storage and Security
- All sensitive data is encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM field-level encryption.
- Authentication tokens are stored securely using platform best practices (e.g., Secure Store, Keychain).
- Data is transmitted securely using HTTPS/TLS.
